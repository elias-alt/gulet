About Us

Gulet Hotel is a premier destination for travelers seeking comfort and convenience. Our hotel offers a range of amenities including well-appointed rooms, a fine dining restaurant, room service, a fitness center, and modern meeting facilities. Whether you're here for business or leisure Gulet Hotel provides exceptional service and a welcoming atmosphere to ensure a memorable stay. Located in a prime area, Gulet Hotel is your ideal home away from home, dedicated to meeting all your needs with excellence.
